ALFREDO

I experimented with Alfredo sauce until I found a quick, cheap, and easy Alfredo sauce combination—the secret ingredient is butter!

Make this easy Alfredo sauce from scratch with butter, milk, and Parmesan. Home cooks love how the butter gives the sauce a rich, smooth texture.

INGRIDIENTS

  • 1 small onion
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 1 cup of ham
  • 1 can of mushrooms
  • 1 cup of cream
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 chicken bouillon cube
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

    • STEPS

    1. Heat oil in a medium, nonstick saucepan over medium heat
    2. Saute onions and garlic. Add ham and mushrooms, sauteing again
    3. Add cream and milk, stir, and bring to a boil
    4. Add pepper to taste
